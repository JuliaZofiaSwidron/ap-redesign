Lifetime pension

With a lifelong pension , the money never runs out, as you are guaranteed an ongoing payment for the rest of your life no matter how long you live. Lifelong pension is your primary product and accounts for 2/3 of your savings. Lifelong pension will be paid to you from the day you retire until you die.
